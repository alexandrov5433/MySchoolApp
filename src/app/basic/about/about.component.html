More about us
Welcome to St. Peter Middle School, a vibrant community dedicated to fostering academic excellence, creativity, and personal growth. Since our founding in 1904, we have been committed to providing a nurturing environment where students are empowered to achieve their full potential. At St. Peter, we believe that education is the cornerstone of success. Our mission is to inspire a love for learning while preparing our students to thrive in an ever-changing world. Guided by our core values of respect, integrity, and perseverance, we create opportunities for every learner to excel both inside and outside the classroom.
We envision a school where innovation meets tradition, and every student feels valued and inspired to dream big. Through a dynamic curriculum and a dedicated team of educators, we strive to equip our students with the skills and confidence they need to make a positive impact in their communities and beyond.
Located in the heart of New Eastwood, St. Peter Middle School is home to a diverse and inclusive community. We celebrate our students' unique talents and cultures, fostering an environment of collaboration and mutual respect. Together, we work to ensure every child feels a sense of belonging and purpose.
Discover the St. Peter Middle School difference. Whether you're a prospective student, parent, or partner, we invite you to visit our campus and experience firsthand the energy, creativity, and spirit that define our school.
